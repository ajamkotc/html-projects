Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Although historians, chefs, and mafia members dispute the origin and truly original recipe of this classic Italian dish, one things remains agreed upon: it's freaking delicious. This recipe follows instructions given by a famous Italian YouTube chef, Vincenzo, and results in simple, quick, and delicious meal. Pair this dish with some earthy red wine, a romantic Italian playlist, and a viewing of the famously Italian movie Moonstruck, and you'll have yourself the perfect date night.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Spaghetti (avoid the yellow, cheap crap)
  • 300g Pancetta, chopped (or Guanciale if you can find it at your local Italian grocer)
  • 3 large eggs + 1 large egg yolk
  • 500g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • (Optional) Red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Cook Spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, place the pancetta or guanciale in a large, cold saucepan. Place on medium low heat and stir regularly.
  3. While the pasta and meat are cooking, whisk together the eggs and cheese to make the cream. Do not add salt, since the cheese is already quite salty.
  4. Whisk in a ladle of pasta water in the cream mixture. This tempers it so that it doesn't turn into scrambled eggs when added to the pan.
  5. Once pancetta has finished cooking, add in pasta, turn off the heat, add cream, and add 1 ladle of pasta water to the mix. Stir continually as the residual heat cooks the cream.
  6. Add red pepper flakes if using, and serve warm.
